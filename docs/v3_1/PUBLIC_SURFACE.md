
v3.1 Public Surface

v3.1 is a repo-front-door release.

It does not claim a new reasoning capability. It compresses the public story around the stable research boundary:

candidate generation != proof
model confidence != proof
typed verifier support = proof boundary
Reader promise

A cold reader should understand TS-Reasoner in under 60 seconds:

candidate proposers generate possible claims;
learned models may rank those claims;
typed verifier channels decide whether claims are accepted, rejected, or abstained;
traces explain the decision;
confidence never becomes proof authority.
Non-claim

v3.1 does not claim broad NLP, general theorem proving, live TensionLM runtime integration, or frontier-model comparison.
